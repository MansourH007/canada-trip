Places To Visit in Canada

From the vibrant streets of Toronto to the majestic snow-capped peaks of the Canadian Rockies, our northern neighbor offers a plethora of vacation destinations that exude beauty throughout the year. Choosing the ideal spot for your next getaway can be challenging. Hence, U.S. News has taken into account various criteria including affordability, entertainment offerings, hotel and resort diversity, alongside user feedback and expert insights, to curate this list of Canada's top destinations to visit.
